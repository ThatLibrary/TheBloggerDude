Guidelines For Getting Listed

I want to list blogs that do not have lots of intrusive ads and blogs that give people value. Search engines like Google and Bing already show lots of massive outlets with intrusive ads and biased information. For that reason, to be listed on TheBloggerDude, I've set up guidelines for your blog to be perfect and suitable for my list.

No Malvertisement

One day I was browsing the web and found a site that was good until I clicked something and it brought up a popup that couldn't be closed, forcing me to restart my PC. That's why if you are a kind-hearted writer with no bad intentions, you should not have any intrusive ads on your blog (popunders, popups, malvertisements). Some known advertisement networks that provide those malvertisements are:

  • ExoClick
  • JuicyAds
  • Clickadv
  • TrafficStars

Google Adsense lets you block catagories of advertisement and you can also block certain ads from showing ads on your site. Give it a try, it'll be worth it for you and your readers.

No Extreme Content

The absolute limit of legitimate news sites is middle-right. You should not have any right-wing radical content on your blog. I cannot support extreme beliefs as such content could harm or influence people in a bad way.
